Our Story

Built for individualized AI integration.

As world pushes businesses to utilize the prowess of AI, we saw how its implementation often slowed businesses down.

The problem doesn't lie within AI itself, rather how it is used: it is never tailored to your needs, business goals, and workflow.

We founded Kinesis AI to fix that: so that businesses can rely on individualized solutions optimized to help you.

Founded 2025Started as two engineers tired of seeing SMBs underserved by big-firm AI consultancies.

What We Stand For

Principles we refuse
to negotiate on.

Radical Transparency

No jargon, no fine print. Every recommendation includes the reason behind it. If something won't work for your business, we'll say so upfront — even if it costs us the project.

Founder Accountability

The people you speak to on the discovery call are the same people who build your solutions. No delegation to juniors, no outsourcing. Your project has names on it.

You Own Everything

When we hand over, you get the code, the credentials, the documentation, and the training. Zero vendor lock-in to us. We succeed when you succeed independently.

Right-Sized Solutions

We match AI complexity to your actual needs. Sometimes that's a sophisticated custom model. Sometimes it's a $20/month tool configured correctly. We'll always tell you which.

Long-Term Thinking

We measure success by what's still working 6 months after handover — not by how impressive the demo looked. Every decision is made with the long run in mind.

No Growth-at-Any-Cost

We turn away work that doesn't fit. We limit intake to protect quality. We'd rather be the best small firm than an average big one. Quality over scale, always.
