Getting Started
What exactly is a free AI audit?

The free audit starts with a 30-minute discovery call where you speak directly with one of our founders. We learn about your business, your current tools, and your biggest efficiency holes. From there, we give you an honest verbal assessment of your top AI opportunities — for free, with no obligation to proceed. If you want a full written scored report, that's our Starter plan.

Will I be speaking to a salesperson on the discovery call?

No. Every discovery call is run personally by one of our two founders — Rishana or Sen. There are no sales reps, no SDRs, no juniors. You'll be talking to the person who will actually do the work if you become a client. That's a deliberate choice on our part.

How quickly can you get started?

Discovery calls are usually available within 3–5 business days. Once you decide to proceed, we begin the formal audit within 48 hours of confirmation. The full Starter audit report is delivered in 5 business days. Growth engagements typically kick off within one week of signing.

Do you work with businesses outside of specific industries?

Yes. Our approach is tailored to our client and can span across retail, legal, healthcare, financial services, real estate, logistics, marketing agencies, and professional services. The core methodology adapts to any business where time is wasted on menial tasks–streamlining workflow is a universal service. If we're ever not the right fit, we'll tell you honestly and refer you to someone who is.

Services & Delivery
What tools and platforms do you build on?

We're tool-agnostic. Most automation work uses Make (Integromat), Zapier, n8n, or custom Python/Node.js depending on complexity and budget. AI systems typically use OpenAI, Anthropic, or open-source models depending on your data privacy requirements. We will always recommend the stack that fits your budget and team's ability to maintain it — not the most impressive-sounding one.

What happens after the project is complete?

You receive full documentation, all credentials, any codebase we've built, and a training session for your team. You own everything outright. We offer optional ongoing support retainers for businesses that want continued access to our founders, but there's never any lock-in or obligation to continue paying us after handover.

How do you handle sensitive business data?

We take data handling very seriously. All client data is covered by a mutual NDA signed before we see anything sensitive. We discuss data residency and privacy requirements during the discovery call, and for regulated industries (healthcare, legal, finance) we can configure solutions to avoid sending sensitive data to external AI providers entirely.

Can you integrate with our existing tools?

Almost certainly yes. We design around what you already use — CRMs, project management tools, accounting platforms, helpdesks, email systems, and more. The goal is to use AI to enhance your workflow, not replace it. We've integrated with Salesforce, HubSpot, Xero, QuickBooks, Zendesk, Notion, Slack, and many others.

Pricing & Contracts
Are there hidden costs I should know about?

No. Our fees are fixed and agreed upfront. The only additional costs are third-party tool subscriptions you'll need to run the solutions we build — we'll scope these out for you in advance so there are no surprises. We never charge overage fees or expand scope without your explicit approval.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

If something isn't working, we want to know immediately. We will work with you to fix it — at our cost, not yours. Our reputation is built on outcomes, and we're personally accountable for every engagement we take on. If we genuinely can't make something work, we'll refund the relevant portion of your fee and explain exactly why.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. For Growth and Transform engagements we typically split payment 50% at kickoff and 50% at handover. For larger custom projects we can arrange milestone-based payment structures. Discuss this with us on the discovery call and we'll figure out something that works for your cash flow.

How is the Transform plan priced?

Transform is scoped individually based on the number of solutions to be built, the complexity of your systems, and the ongoing support level you need. We typically quote a monthly retainer after the initial discovery and audit. Most Transform clients find the ROI from the first month's automation savings more than covers the retainer. We'll always show you the maths before asking you to commit.
