Consulting Services

Everything your team needs to use AI well

Strategy, systems design, and light implementation — without needing to hire engineers or become a software company.

01

AI Readiness & Workflow Audit

Map how work actually gets done today: who does what, where information gets stuck, and which tasks are truly repetitive. You receive a clear picture of where AI can safely and meaningfully help.

  • Process mapping sessions with key team members
  • Inventory of repetitive and decision-heavy tasks
  • Prioritized AI opportunities and "do not automate" zones
02

AI Strategy & Roadmap

Turn that audit into a practical plan: which use cases to start with, what tools fit your stack and budget, and how to phase changes so the team isn't overwhelmed.

  • 1–3 quick‑win projects scoped with timelines
  • Medium‑term initiatives for deeper impact
  • Risk, data, and change‑management considerations
03

Workflow & Communication Design

Design future‑state workflows that blend humans and AI: when to use tools like Claude or ChatGPT, how information should move, and what "good" looks like for your team.

  • Redesigned workflows and communication flows
  • Shared prompt libraries and templates
  • Roles and responsibilities for humans vs AI assistants
04

Tool Selection & Light Implementation

Help choosing and configuring the right tools — no heavy custom coding. You keep ownership; technical partners or in-house IT can implement deeper changes as needed.

  • Shortlisted tools matched to your use cases
  • Implementation checklist and success criteria
  • Team training and onboarding material
05

Knowledge & Documentation Systems

Build internal systems where information is easy to find, summarize, and reuse with AI: better notes, playbooks, and documented processes your team will actually maintain.

  • Structure for internal knowledge bases
  • Standards for documentation and updates
  • Guidelines for safe AI use on internal content
06

Ongoing Advisory

As tools and your business evolve, Plasma AI acts as a sounding board: sense‑checking ideas, refining prompts, and continuously improving workflows over time.

  • Monthly or quarterly review sessions
  • Feedback from your team translated into improvements
  • Updates as AI capabilities and best practices shift

Questions You Might Have

Common questions

Do you build custom software?

Plasma AI focuses on strategy, process design, and light implementation guidance. For heavy engineering or full custom platforms, we help you define requirements and can hand off to your developers or trusted partners.

Which tools do you work with?

Tool‑agnostic by design. Depending on your needs, you might use Claude, ChatGPT, Notion AI, Zapier, Make, CRM automations, or other platforms that fit your stack and data requirements.

How long does an engagement take?

A focused audit and roadmap typically takes 2–4 weeks from discovery to final recommendations, with optional ongoing advisory afterwards.

Do I need a technical team?

No. Plasma AI is specifically designed for teams without dedicated developers. We recommend tools and workflows your team can operate and maintain themselves, with clear handoff documentation.
